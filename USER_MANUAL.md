# 📖 Wehu CBC SMS — Official User Manual

Welcome to the **Wehu CBC School Management System**, a premium digital platform designed specifically for the Kenya Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). This guide will help you navigate and master the system's core features.

---

## 1. Getting Started
### Accessing the Portal
1. Navigate to your school's unique URL (e.g., `http://localhost:5173`).
2. You will be greeted by the **Secure Access Portal**.
3. Enter your **School Identity (Email)** and **Security Token (Password)**.
4. Click **Access Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Academic CBC Engine (For Teachers)
The heart of the system is the academic assessment matrix.

### Marking Assessments
- Go to **Academic CBC** in the sidebar.
- Select your **Grade**, **Stream**, and **Subject**.
- You will see the CBC Learning Outcomes for the current term.
- Rate students using the official scale:
  - **EE**: Exceeding Expectations
  - **ME**: Meeting Expectations
  - **AE**: Approaching Expectations
  - **BE**: Below Expectations

### Generating Weekly Reports
- The system automatically aggregates assessment data every Friday.
- You can review these in the **Progress Portal** to see automated strength/weakness analysis.

---

## 3. Financial Management (For Bursars)
### Recording Payments
1. Go to **Finance & Billing**.
2. Search for the student by name or admission number.
3. Enter the amount paid, payment method (Cash/M-Pesa/Bank), and the reference number.
4. The system will instantly update the student's balance and payment status (Paid/Partial).

### Arrears Management
- Use the **Arrears List** tab to see all students with pending balances.
- You can filter this list by Grade or Stream for targeted follow-ups.

---

## 4. Administrative Controls (For School Leadership)
### School-Wide Announcements
- Navigate to the **Control Center**.
- Select the **Broadcast Tools** tab.
- Compose your message and select the target audience (e.g., All Parents).
- Choose your delivery channels: **SMS**, **Email**, or **Push Notification**.

### End-of-Term Report Cards
- Go to **Report Cards**.
- Sync assessment data for your stream.
- Add Class Teacher and Principal remarks.
- Once approved, click **Generate PDF** to release reports to the Parent Portal.

---

## 5. Parent & Student Portal
### Child Progress
- Parents can switch between multiple children using the profile cards at the top.
- View real-time attendance, fee balances, and recent academic ratings.
- Download official Report Cards directly to your device once released.

---

## 6. Offline Mode & PWA
### Installing the App
- In Chrome/Edge, click the **Install** icon in the address bar.
- On Mobile, use **"Add to Home Screen"**.
- This allows you to access the dashboard even when the school's internet is unstable.

---

## 💡 Troubleshooting
- **Forgotten Password**: Contact the System Administrator (`admin@wehu.ac.ke`) for a reset.
- **Sync Issues**: If data doesn't appear immediately, check the **Offline Alert** at the bottom. If you are offline, your changes will sync once connection is restored.
